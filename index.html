About Me

Hi! I'm Mickey!

I'm a multimedia artist who honestly goes into making things right away not knowing what I want to create, not knowing what I want to do, but just finding my way through color and passion. I love to hold a pencil, I love to get my face close to the paper and let the work unfold itself.

I think I paint through emotion because I don't like thinking about comparing my work to other artists or thinking about what will "sell". I like going into a painting knowing that what I'm creating is fully for me, and if someone along the way also sees something that speaks to them through my art, my heart is whole.

I love to create not only for myself, but to impact the world around me. I love to think that someone out there may find a hidden message between the strokes of paint put on a canvas.

I find myself with dozens of unfinished artworks, and these are pieces that, in their incompleteness, become some of my favorite pieces to this day.

Both in my life and in my art, I have to allow myself to focus on the things within my control and let the rest go. As I remind myself often with a quote by someone I admire deeply:

“I can only change what I can change.”
